Position

We collect small, specific, and authentic moments from personal lives—things that won't make headlines but shape who we are.

OMMFA is a not-for-profit, decentralised arts organisation, based between London and Shanghai. Since 2020, we have worked across exhibitions, publications, and online projects, often without a fixed venue. Recent work includes The Wrong Biennale (2025/26), alongside ongoing publications and field notes. We are currently working towards THE WAY at London Design Festival 2026 and a return to the Edinburgh Fringe.

Our work is created for readers, viewers, and contributors who care about the details of contemporary life, and for the artists and writers who first noticed these details.

As a non-profit initiative, we dedicate all contributions entirely to supporting our artists and writers, and to realising these independent projects
