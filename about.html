Position

We collect small, specific, and authentic moments from personal lives—things that won't make headlines but shape our lives.

OMMFA is a decentralised arts organisation, based between London and Shanghai. Since 2020, we have worked across exhibitions, publications, and online projects, often without a fixed venue. Recent work includes The Wrong Biennale (2025/26) and the Edinburgh Fringe (2025), alongside ongoing publications and field notes.

Our work is created for readers, viewers, and contributors who care about the details of contemporary life, and for the artists and writers who first noticed these details.
