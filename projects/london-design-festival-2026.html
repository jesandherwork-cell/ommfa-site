Some moments don't announce themselves. You take one street instead of another, you stay five minutes longer, you say yes without thinking. Nothing seems to happen — and then, later, you realise something had already changed.

THE WAY invites work that rests on one of those moments: a small, unnoticed turn that quietly redirected a life. Not cause and effect made dramatic, not the butterfly that starts a storm — just the ordinary fork you only recognised once you'd already passed it. The way out, the way in, the way to.

We're showing these together as a non-linear conversation — many turns, side by side, with no single route through.

What we're looking for. Work that rests on one specific, unnoticed turn. It can be unfinished, unclear, a fragment. Any medium welcome: painting, print, sculpture, installation, performance especially, sound, moving image, interactive and interdisciplinary work.

How it works. Submissions are reviewed on a rolling basis and space is limited, so earlier is better. You'll hear back within 10 working days. If you're not selected, we'll send you a short note explaining why — we want to respect every artist's work.

Shipping. Selected artists are responsible for getting their work to the London venue. For print-based work, we can arrange printing in the UK — so you only need to send the file.
