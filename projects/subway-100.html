Train carriages are one of the few places where we can still do nothing.

In 2020, OMMFA opened a shared document. It posed a simple question: On the subway, besides looking at our phones, what else can we do? People in our Moments started leaving comments—friends, former colleagues, and strangers. After reaching one hundred comments, it stopped.

The 99th comment was, "Send 100 copies to people around you." The 100th comment was, "Report the person who sent the copies." Finally, the list was automatically archived, and we liked it ending like that.

Presented by OMMFA as an online exhibition, this page archives that single-use collaborative document exactly as it happened. It was a one-time event in 2020. We didn't keep it going. It happened, and then it was over.
